Welcome to Igor Gorlov's Coding Style Documentation

In all times, people have been needing to unify their goals and methods in order to collaborate effectively. The legend of the Tower of Babel can be considered an example of the contrary approach that had led to an epic fail. The simplest (and probably the most reliable) way to accomplish unification is defining common standards everyone should follow. This collection of documents is nothing but a try to maintain consistency and readability of the code we write together. I hope you will take that matter seriously.
